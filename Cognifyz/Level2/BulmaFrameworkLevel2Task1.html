Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir, also known as the Rama Temple, is a Hindu temple located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India. According to Hindu belief, it stands at the site of Ram Janmabhoomi, the mythical birthplace of Lord Rama, a principal deity in Hinduism. The temple complex, currently under construction, is being supervised by the Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra Trust. On January 22, 2024, Prime Minister Narendra Modi performed the prana pratishtha of the temple.
